Lemon Pound Cake Recipe

The entry-level version is a zero-failure cake!

Pound cake is a very basic and classic cake. It does not require too many skills to make, and is very suitable for beginners to practice.

Those who have eaten it know that it has a solid taste and is very filling. The aroma of lemon in the house lingers all day longðŸ¤—

INGREDIENTS:

Cake base

  • 200g butter
  • 160g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 1tbs lemon zest
  • 180g flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 120ml milk
  • 3tbs lemon juice

Icing sugar

  • 90g powdered sugar
  • 2tbs lemon juice

STEPS:

Follow as below and you won't miss it!

  1. Preheat the oven to 165 degrees, grease the mold with oil and powder for later use.
  2. In a bowl, add room temperature butter and sugar, and beat with a mixer at medium speed until smooth, about 3-4 minutes. If the butter is too hard, you can put a bowl with hot water and beat it over water.
  3. Add 3 eggs, beat with the mixer for another minute until mixed, add vanilla extract and lemon zest;
  4. Take another bowl, pour in flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt, mix well, then pour half of the powder into the butter bowl and mix;
  5. Pour in half of the milk and lemon juice and stir well;
  6. Repeat, pour in the other half of the dry powder and mix; then pour in the remaining milk and stir with a spatula or mixer. Be careful not to over-mix.
  7. Pour the batter into the mold and bake at 165 degrees for 50 minutes.
  8. Let it cool after taking it out, and stir the powdered sugar and lemon juice until it is fluid.
  9. Pour the frosting on the surface of the cake and sprinkle some lemon zest for decoration.

Done!

TIPS:

  • Soft the butter at room temperature in advance to make it easier to mix.
  • Please adjust the time slightly according to the actual temperature of the oven. After baking, insert a toothpick into the cake. When it is taken out, it is clean and has no attachments.
  • If you want the cracks in the middle to be even and beautiful, take the cake out of the oven after 15 minutes of baking, draw a line in the middle, and the middle will naturally crack during baking.
  • The taste will be more moist and soft after overnight.
